About Urban Threads

Urban Threads is a fictional clothing store located in Cape Town, South Africa. It opened its doors in 2020 and has since become known for offering stylish, affordable clothing aimed at young adults. The store’s mission is simple: provide trendy, high-quality pieces that capture the energy of urban culture. Looking ahead, Urban Threads hopes to branch into online sales so it can reach a wider market. Its main customers are fashion-minded individuals between the ages of 18 and 35.

Mission

Make quality streetwear accessible without compromising style.

Vision

Become the go‑to South African streetwear staple for 18–35s.
